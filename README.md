# Elaria JDR Project Documentation

## Overview
Elaria is a tabletop role-playing game (JDR) that immerses players into a rich world filled with adventure, magic, and exploration. This document provides detailed information about the game mechanics, character creation, and lore that brings Elaria to life.

📚 Règles JDR (source) : voir [`ELARIA-JDR/README.md`](ELARIA-JDR/README.md).

## Game Mechanics
- **Dice System**: The game uses a d20 system for most actions. Players roll a 20-sided die to determine the outcome of their actions.
- **Attributes**: Characters have six main attributes: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.
- **Skills**: Each character can choose skills that enhance their abilities in specific areas, such as Stealth, Persuasion, and Arcana.

## Character Creation
1. **Choose a Race**: Options include Human, Elf, Dwarf, and more.
2. **Select a Class**: Classes such as Warrior, Mage, and Rogue define the character's abilities.
3. **Allocate Attributes**: Players distribute points to define their character's strengths and weaknesses.
4. **Background**: Choose a background that gives context and advantages.

## Lore
Elaria is divided into various regions, each with its own culture and history. Key regions include the Mystic Forest, the Ruins of Eldar, and the City of Arcanis.

- **Magic**: The world is rich in magic, sourced from ancient artifacts and ley lines.
- **Factions**: Various factions vie for power, including the Brotherhood of Shadows and the Guardians of the Light.

## How to Play
- Gather a group of friends, appoint a Game Master (GM), and embark on your adventure in Elaria!
- Use this document as a reference to navigate through quests, encounters, and character development.
